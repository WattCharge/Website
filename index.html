Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is WattCharge?

    WattCharge, a subsidiary of Sinatra company, stands as a leading electric car charging service dedicated to providing convenient and flexible charging solutions. Whether you need an emergency charge, want to schedule in advance, or require a portable power solution, WattCharge, backed by the expertise of Sinatra company, is here to empower your electric journey.

  • When is WattCharge launching?

    WattCharge is set to launch on February 4th, 2024, marking the beginning of a new era in sustainable and hassle-free electric vehicle charging.

  • What subscription plans does WattCharge offer for scheduled charging?

    WattCharge offers flexible subscription plans, including weekly and monthly options. Choose the plan that suits your needs and enjoy the convenience of pre-booking your charging appointments.

  • Is WattCharge compatible with all electric vehicles?

    Yes, WattCharge is designed to be compatible with a wide range of electric vehicles. Our goal is to provide a universal charging solution, ensuring accessibility for all EV owners.

  • Is WattCharge available for commercial fleets or businesses?

    Absolutely! WattCharge offers customized solutions for commercial fleets and businesses. Contact our business services team through the app or website to discuss your specific requirements and explore partnership opportunities.

  • What payment methods are accepted at WattCharge mobile stations?

    WattCharge accepts various payment methods, including credit cards and mobile payment options. You can view the accepted payment methods and manage your transactions within the WattCharge app.

  • How can I contact WattCharge customer support?

    For any inquiries or assistance, reach out to our dedicated customer support team through the WattCharge app or visit our Contact Us page on the website.

  • How can I provide feedback or report an issue with a WattCharge mobile station?

    Your feedback is valuable! Use the WattCharge app to report any issues or provide feedback on your charging experience. Our customer support team will address your concerns promptly.
